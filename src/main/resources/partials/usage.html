Using KeYmaera 4

The page "Your Models" lists the available models and allows you to upload new models. You can list the proofs of a model by clicking on "Show proofs", and start a new proof by selecting "Start new proof" from the drop-down-box.

Click on the model name to look at the model. Click on the description to get additional information.

The page "Your Proofs" lists the available proofs and allows you to load and reprove. While reproving, a spinning wheel indicates that the prover is busy. When reproving finished, the spinning wheel changes into a button that lets you open the proof.

The proof view indicates your tasks in a proof agenda. Right of or below the agenda, depending on your screen resolution, you can find the proof obligation of the selected agenda item. Hover the mouse over formulas in the proof obligation to see your options for proof search locally at that formula. Step ( Step) performs a single proof step based on the top-level operator in that formula (here, it will execute a propositional tactic handling implication). Hide ( Hide) performs weakening, which means it removes the formula from the proof obligation.

Instead of automated proof search, you can manually interact with the prover by clicking an operator in a formula (e.g., the implication symbol). This opens a popup dialog that displays the applicable tactics. Click a tactic button to execute that tactic on the formula displayed at the top of the dialog (this is the formula selected when opening the dialog).

To let KeYmaera search for a proof fully automated, hover the mouse over the turnstile symbol. This will show automated tactics to handle all kinds of formulas ( Auto), only propositional tactics ( Propositional), tactics to handle arithmetic ( Arithmetic), or automated tactics without making arithmetic steps, such as quantifier elimination ( w/o Arith.).
